About Us

We are talented house music artist based in pretoria tshawne mamelodi west.

Mams Blaq Project (MBP) is a registered indipendent music art entertainment company. we love connecting people together to share and showcase our talent and skill to outside world.

M B P founded by Sibusiso Kekana stage name Dj Terry De Za in 2011 june 15 follwed by Thanbang Malobola a close friend with Sibusiso Kekana. These two shared one thing in common which is music driven by burning desire, Passion and talent.

Being indepent in the music industry need to be organized, Working with different people exchanging ideas is our strategy to expand the company. today we are able to market our own music production and we looking forward to work with popular artist and grow the company

  • Mission - To deliver the best service with iffecient time trust and contract the operations between the company and the client.

  • Vision - To see this company in the next 2 years as a leading and best service delivering with braches in Pretoria and Johnessburg.

  • Small Term Goal - To have 1 million users in 2018.
  • Medium Term Goal - To have 10 office branches 5 in pretoria 5 in Johnessburg in 5 years time..
  • Long Term Goal - To be the best recomended music art entertainment company in South Africa
